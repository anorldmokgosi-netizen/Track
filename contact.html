Safari Planning FAQs

Quick answers to help plan your Botswana safari adventure

What is the best time to visit Botswana for a safari?

The dry season (May to October) is generally considered the best time for safaris in Botswana. During this period, wildlife congregates around water sources, making animals easier to spot. The weather is pleasant with little to no rainfall. However, the green season (November to April) offers beautiful landscapes, migratory bird viewing, and lower prices with fewer tourists.

How far in advance should I book my safari?

We recommend booking at least 6-12 months in advance for peak season (June-October) to secure preferred accommodations and dates. For shoulder seasons, 3-6 months is usually sufficient. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible, but availability for premium camps and lodges can be limited.

What safety measures do you have in place?

Your safety is our top priority. All our guides are professionally trained and experienced in wilderness first aid. We maintain small group sizes (max 7 guests), carry satellite communication devices in remote areas, follow strict park regulations, and conduct comprehensive safety briefings. Our vehicles are well-maintained and equipped with first aid kits and emergency supplies.

What is included in your safari packages?

Our safari packages typically include accommodation, all meals, game drives, park fees, professional guide services, airport transfers, and most activities. Exclusions usually include international flights, travel insurance, visa fees, personal purchases, and gratuities. Each package has a detailed inclusion list on its specific page.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we can accommodate most dietary restrictions with advance notice. Please inform us of any dietary requirements (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, allergies, etc.) when booking. Our camps and lodges are experienced in catering to various dietary needs while maintaining delicious and nutritious meals in the bush.

What makes TRACKS different from other safari companies?

As a 100% Botswana-owned company, we offer authentic local expertise combined with premium service standards. Our signature safari routes are carefully curated based on years of experience and deep knowledge of Botswana's ecosystems. We specialize in predator-focused safaris and maintain small groups for personalized attention. Our commitment to sustainable tourism supports local communities and conservation efforts.
