Journey Through Liquid Emerald

The Emerald Waterways Expedition offers an intimate exploration of Botswana's Okavango Delta, the world's largest inland delta. This water-based safari takes you through crystal-clear channels, tranquil lagoons, and lush islands teeming with wildlife.

Experience the unique perspective of traveling by mokoro (traditional dugout canoe) and motorboat, getting closer to wildlife than ever before. From hippos and crocodiles in the waterways to elephants crossing the channels and lions on the islands, this safari offers unforgettable encounters with Africa's most iconic species.

Water-Based Exploration

Travel by mokoro and motorboat through pristine waterways inaccessible by vehicle.

Luxury Island Camps

Stay at exclusive private island camps with stunning delta views and premium amenities.

Aquatic Wildlife

Spot hippos, crocodiles, otters, and abundant birdlife in their natural habitat.

Land & Water Game

Experience both water-based activities and traditional land-based game drives.

Delta Highlights

Experience the unique wonders of the Okavango Delta ecosystem

Traditional Mokoro Safaris

Glide silently through narrow channels in traditional dugout canoes (mokoros), getting close to wildlife without disturbing them. The perfect way to experience the delta's tranquility.

Traditional Silent Intimate

Aquatic Giants

Observe hippo pods in their natural aquatic environment and watch Nile crocodiles basking on riverbanks. Learn about these fascinating creatures from expert guides.

Hippos Crocodiles Aquatic Life

Birdwatcher's Paradise

Delta Activities

Experience the Okavango Delta through unique water and land activities

Mokoro Safaris

Traditional dugout canoe trips through narrow channels with expert polers, offering silent approach to wildlife.

Motorboat Safaris

Explore deeper channels and larger water bodies to access remote areas and spot aquatic wildlife.

Land Game Drives

4x4 game drives on larger islands to track big game including elephant, buffalo, lion, and leopard.

Walking Safaris

Guided walks on islands to experience the delta's smaller wonders and learn tracking skills.

Fishing

Catch and release fishing for tigerfish, bream, and catfish in designated fishing areas.

Birdwatching

Specialist birding safaris focusing on the delta's 400+ bird species with expert bird guides.
