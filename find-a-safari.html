Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our safaris

What's included in the safari price?

All our safaris include: accommodation in quality tents, all meals, park fees, professional guide, safari vehicle, camping equipment, and activities as per itinerary. Exclusions: international flights, visas, travel insurance, personal expenses, and gratuities.

What's the difference between signature and predator routes?

Signature safaris focus on specific ecosystems (Delta waterways, desert landscapes, northern rivers). Predator routes follow wildlife corridors between regions, with specialized guides and itineraries optimized for predator tracking and viewing. Both offer excellent wildlife experiences.

What is the maximum group size?

We maintain a maximum of 7 guests per safari to ensure personalized attention, minimal environmental impact, and optimal wildlife viewing opportunities for everyone.

When is the best time to visit Botswana?

Dry season (May-October) offers the best wildlife viewing as animals congregate around water sources. Green season (November-April) has lush landscapes, bird migrations, and fewer tourists. We operate year-round with dual-season pricing.

What level of fitness is required?

Our safaris are suitable for most fitness levels. Some walking is involved on nature walks, but these are optional and at a gentle pace. Please advise us of any mobility concerns when booking.

Need Help Choosing?

Our safari experts are here to help you select the perfect Botswana adventure. Contact us for personalized recommendations and detailed information about any safari.
