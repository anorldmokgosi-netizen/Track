The Reverse Corridor Experience

This unique itinerary offers a different perspective on Botswana's wildlife migration patterns. By starting in the dry savannah of Savuti and moving toward the lush Delta forests, you'll witness changing ecosystems and predator adaptations along the way.

Dry to Wet Transition

Experience ecosystems changing from arid savannah to lush forests

Fewer Tourists
