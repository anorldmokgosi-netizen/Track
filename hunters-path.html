Ultimate Predator Experience

The Hunters' Path is a specialized 6-night safari designed for wildlife enthusiasts seeking intimate predator encounters. This expedition takes you deep into Botswana's most predator-rich ecosystems: the dense forests of Moremi Game Reserve and the wildlife-rich floodplains of Khwai Concession.

With expert trackers who understand predator behavior, you'll follow the trails of leopards, lions, and wild dogs in their natural habitats. This safari focuses on quality sightings, behavioral observation, and understanding the intricate balance of Botswana's predator-prey relationships.

Leopard Tracking

Specialized tracking of elusive leopards in their prime forest and riverine habitats.

Lion Behavior

Observe lion pride dynamics, hunting strategies, and social interactions in open plains.

Wild Dog Packs

Follow Africa's most endangered predator with the highest hunting success rate.

Expert Guides

Local guides with specialized knowledge of predator movements and behavior patterns.

Safari Itinerary

7-day journey through Botswana's predator corridor

Day 1
Arrival in Moremi Game Reserve
  • Meet your expert predator guide at Maun Airport
  • Transfer into Moremi Game Reserve's eastern woodlands
  • First predator-focused game drive en route to camp
  • Arrival at secluded wilderness camp in prime leopard territory
  • Predator tracking briefing and safety orientation
  • Welcome dinner under the African stars
Day 2
Moremi: Leopard & Forest Predators
  • Early morning tracking of leopard movements
  • Explore dense riverine forests and mopane woodlands
  • Focus on ambush predator behavior and strategies
  • Brunch followed by midday photography session
  • Afternoon game drive in prime wild dog territory
  • Evening predator ecology discussion
Day 3
Xakanaxa: Predator Hotspot
  • Full day exploring Xakanaxa's predator-rich ecosystems
  • Morning focus on lion pride dynamics
  • Bird of prey photography opportunities
  • Afternoon tracking lessons with expert guide
  • Sunset drinks overlooking predator hunting grounds
  • Night sounds and predator vocalizations
Day 4
Transfer to Khwai Concession
  • Morning game drive transfer through wildlife corridor
  • Predator activity along the transition zone
  • Arrival at Khwai River wilderness camp
  • Settle into new camp overlooking prime predator territory
  • Evening game drive along Khwai River floodplains
  • Dinner with focus on Khwai's predator populations
Day 5
Khwai: Lion & Wild Dog Focus
  • Early morning search for wild dog packs
  • Follow hunting trails and den sites
  • Brunch and predator behavior analysis
  • Afternoon focus on lion pride interactions
  • Golden hour photography on open floodplains
  • Optional night drive for nocturnal predators
Day 6
Magotho: Predator Finale
  • Morning game drive focusing on hyena clans

  • Predator-prey relationship observations

  • Conservation discussion and future tracking
Day 7
Departure
  • Early morning tea and final predator tracking

  • Departure with unforgettable predator memories
  • Digital photo sharing and conservation resources

Predator Activities

Specialized activities focused on predator observation and tracking

Predator Tracking

Learn traditional tracking skills from expert guides. Follow spoor, interpret signs, and understand predator movement patterns in different habitats.

Behavioral Observation

Extended observation sessions to understand predator social structures, hunting strategies, and daily routines without disturbance.

Predator Photography

Specialized photography focusing on predator behavior, interactions, and portraits. Learn techniques for low-light and action shots.

Night Drives

Explore the nocturnal world of predators. Spotlights reveal nighttime hunters including leopards, hyenas, and smaller predators.

Conservation Insights

Learn about predator conservation efforts, research projects, and community involvement in protecting Botswana's predators.

Expert Guidance

Daily briefings and discussions with guides who have specialized knowledge of predator ecology and individual animal histories.

Predator Destinations

Journey through Botswana's most predator-rich wilderness areas

Moremi Game Reserve

The "Predator Capital of Botswana" with dense forests perfect for leopards, riverine habitats for lions, and open areas for wild dog packs. Highest predator density in the Okavango Delta.

Leopard Wild Dog Lion Hyena Cheetah

Khwai Concession

A community-managed wildlife corridor between Moremi and Chobe. The Khwai River attracts incredible wildlife concentrations, making it prime territory for predators following migratory patterns.

Lion Leopard Wild Dog Hyena Jackal
