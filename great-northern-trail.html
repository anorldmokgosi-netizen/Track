Follow the traditional wildlife migration route from the lush Okavango Delta through the wildlife-rich Khwai corridor to the dramatic Savuti Marsh. This classic itinerary offers the quintessential Botswana safari experience, following the paths used by generations of wildlife.

Detailed Itinerary

Day-by-day breakdown of your classic predator corridor safari

Day 1
Arrive in Moremi
  • Meet guide in Maun, drive into Moremi
  • Game drive en route to camp
  • Settle into Xakanaxa area camp
  • Welcome dinner and safari briefing
Day 2
Xakanaxa & Okavango Fringe
  • Explore iconic floodplains
  • Leopard hotspot tracking
  • Evergreen forest exploration
  • Birding in diverse habitats
Day 3
Black Pools & Dead Tree Island
  • Full day in predator-rich areas
  • Wild dog territory exploration
  • Lion pride tracking
  • Eastern Moremi ecosystems
Day 4
Transfer to Khwai
  • Scenic drive through mopane forests
  • Arrive in Khwai mid-afternoon
  • Evening game drive along river
  • Community area introduction
Day 5
Khwai Floodplains
  • Leopards, lions, elephants
  • Red lechwe and sable antelope
  • River channel exploration
  • Optional night drive
Day 6
Woodland Exploration
  • Mixed habitat predator search
  • Wild dog and hyena tracking
  • Birding in riverine forests
  • Cultural interaction opportunities
Day 7
Transfer to Savuti
  • Drive through Mababe Depression
  • Enter dramatic Savuti Marsh
  • Afternoon game drive in marsh
  • Lions and hyenas territory
Day 8
Savuti Marsh & Baobab Hills
  • Morning in the legendary marsh
  • Afternoon at Bushman Rock
  • Baobab area exploration
  • Elephant herds observation
Day 9
Secret Waterholes & Predator Activity
  • Explore remote pans
  • Lion territory tracking
  • Hyena clan observations
  • Farewell Savuti dinner
Day 10
Ends in Kasane
  • Early morning game drive
  • Exit Chobe National Park
  • Transfer to Kasane
  • Depart with incredible memories
