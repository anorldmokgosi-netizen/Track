Rivers of the North Safari

A sweeping 12-day/11-night expedition across Botswana's legendary waterways. From the bird-rich Okavango Panhandle to the predator-thick Savuti Marsh and the elephant kingdom of Chobe, this route delivers unforgettable moments every single day.

12 Days / 11 Nights
Max 7 Guests

Botswana's Ultimate River Journey

The Rivers of the North Safari is our most comprehensive expedition, covering Botswana's iconic northern waterways in one epic journey. This 11-night safari takes you from the tranquil Okavango Panhandle through the predator-rich Kwando region, into the legendary Savuti Marsh, and finally to the elephant paradise of Chobe Riverfront.

Experience the dramatic contrasts of Botswana's northern ecosystems — from peaceful river cruises to thrilling predator encounters, from bird-watching paradises to some of Africa's best elephant viewing. This safari is designed for travelers who want to experience the full spectrum of Botswana's northern wilderness in one unforgettable journey.

Water Wilderness

Explore three of Botswana's greatest river systems in one trip — Okavango, Kwando, and Chobe.

A Predator's World

Follow the movements of lions, leopards, and painted wolves (wild dogs) across different habitats.

Elephant Kingdom

Experience the highest elephant densities in Africa along the Chobe Riverfront.

Photographer's Dream

Golden light, dramatic landscapes, diverse wildlife, and perfect river reflections.

Safari Itinerary

12-day journey through Botswana's legendary northern waterways

Day 1
Maun → Okavango Panhandle
  • Meet your guide at Maun Airport
  • Scenic northern transfer to Okavango Panhandle
  • Arrival at riverside camp overlooking the Panhandle
  • Afternoon boat safari introduction
  • Bird watching paradise - fish eagles, herons, kingfishers
  • Welcome dinner and safari briefing
Day 2
Panhandle Boat & Island Walks
  • Morning boat safari through deep channels
  • Guided island walk focusing on riverine ecology
  • Fish eagle sightings and photography
  • Hippo pool observations from safe distance
  • Afternoon relaxation at camp
  • Sunset river cruise with sundowners
Day 3
Transfer to Kwando River
  • Morning game drive transfer through forested landscapes
  • Elephant corridors and sightings en route
  • Arrival at Kwando River wilderness camp
  • Afternoon setup and orientation
  • Evening game drive along Kwando River
  • Dinner under the stars
Day 4
Kwando Predator Zone
  • Full day exploring Kwando's predator-rich areas
  • Track big cats - lion and leopard territories
  • Wild dog territory exploration
  • Riverine woodlands and open plains contrast
  • Brunch in the bush followed by siesta
  • Evening focus on nocturnal species
Day 5
Kwando Exploration
  • Morning birding and mammal photography
  • Focus on Kwando's unique bird species
  • Afternoon boat cruise on Kwando River
  • Crocodile and hippo sightings
  • Sunset over the waterways
  • Night drive for nocturnal predators
Day 6
Kwando → Savuti
  • Game drive transfer through beautiful wilderness
  • Wildlife sightings en route to Savuti
  • Arrival at iconic Savuti Marsh area
  • Camp setup in Savuti wilderness
  • Evening game drive in legendary Savuti
  • Campfire stories of Savuti's dramatic history
Day 7
Savuti Marsh Exploration
  • Full day dedicated to Savuti's unique ecosystem
  • Early morning predator tracking at waterholes
  • Focus on lion and hyena clan interactions
  • Photography at iconic baobab landscapes
  • Brunch in the bush overlooking the marsh
  • Afternoon exploration of dry riverbeds
  • Evening game drive for nocturnal activity
Day 8
Savuti → Chobe
  • Morning game drive transfer towards Chobe
  • Riverbank wildlife sightings en route
  • Arrival at Chobe Riverfront area
  • Afternoon setup at riverside camp
  • Evening river cruise on Chobe River
  • Dinner overlooking the river
Day 9
Chobe Riverfront
  • Morning game drive along Chobe Riverfront
  • Elephants in huge numbers drinking and bathing
  • Buffalo herds and predator interactions
  • Birdlife viewing - specialized river species
  • Afternoon river cruise
  • Sunset photography on the river
Day 10
Chobe Deep Exploration
  • Full day exploring different sections of Chobe
  • Focus on elephant family group behaviors
  • Bird watching for rare species
  • Riverine forest walks (where permitted)
  • Photography of elephant swimming scenes
  • Sunset river reflections photography
Day 11
Final Safari
  • Early morning game drive for final sightings
  • Focus on photography and special requests
  • Return to camp for celebration brunch
  • Afternoon at leisure at camp
  • Closing evening game drive
  • Farewell campfire dinner
Day 12
Kasane Departure
  • Early morning tea and light breakfast
  • Transfer to Kasane Airport
  • Final wildlife sightings en route
  • Arrival at Kasane Airport by mid-morning
  • Assistance with check-in and departures
  • Depart with unforgettable river memories

River Destinations

Journey through Botswana's most iconic northern river systems

Okavango Panhandle

A peaceful stretch of the Delta known for birds, hippos, and deep channels. Perfect for boat safaris and bird watching in tranquil surroundings.

Hippo Crocodile Fish Eagle Heron Otter Kingfisher

Kwando River

Known for predators, large elephant herds, and dramatic sightings. The Kwando supports some of Africa's highest predator densities.

Lion Wild Dog Leopard Elephant Roan Sable

Savuti Marsh

A world-famous predator arena with dramatic wildlife behavior. The mysterious Savuti Channel creates unique ecosystems and incredible sightings.

Hyena Lion Leopard Elephant Giraffe Zebra

Chobe Riverfront

Home to the world's greatest elephant populations and spectacular sunset cruises. The Chobe River supports incredible biodiversity.

Elephant Buffalo Sable Puku Crocodile Birds

River Activities

Experience northern Botswana through diverse safari activities

River Cruises

Boat safaris on Okavango Panhandle, Kwando River, and Chobe River for different perspectives on aquatic wildlife and bird life.

Game Drives

Morning and evening drives in open 4x4 vehicles across diverse habitats from riverine forests to open plains and marshlands.

Predator Tracking

Specialized tracking of lions, leopards, wild dogs, and hyenas across different northern Botswana ecosystems.

Riverfront Viewing

Spend time at riverbanks observing elephant herds, hippo pods, crocodiles, and diverse bird species coming to drink.

Photographic Safaris

Focus on wildlife behavior, landscapes, river reflections, and golden light photography across four different regions.

Campfire Evenings

Relax around campfires sharing stories of each day's discoveries and listening to the sounds of the African night.
